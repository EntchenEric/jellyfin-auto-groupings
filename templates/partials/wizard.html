Let's get your Jellyfin library organized in just a few steps.

How it works

This tool creates "Virtual Libraries" in Jellyfin by using symlinks on your hard drive. It doesn't move or copy your actual files, so it's completely safe! You can group movies by genres, actors, years, or even external lists from IMDb and Trakt.
